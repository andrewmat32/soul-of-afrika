Trip Highlights:

  •  Witness the Great Migration across the Serengeti plains
  •  Luxury accommodations in the heart of nature
  •  Expert-guided safari drives, including night safaris
  •  Private bush dinners under the stars
  •  Hot air balloon rides for breathtaking aerial views
  •  Up-close encounters with the Big Five

Embark on an unforgettable 14-day journey across Botswana's pristine landscapes, following the awe-inspiring migration of wildebeests and zebras. This exclusive safari offers a front-row seat to one of nature’s most breathtaking spectacles, where you’ll witness the dramatic rhythms of wildlife migration in some of the world’s most iconic national parks. Traverse vast, untouched wilderness areas, from the Okavango Delta's lush wetlands to the sweeping plains of the Kalahari Desert.

With luxurious accommodations nestled in the heart of the wilderness, enjoy personalized service that ensures every moment of your adventure is crafted to perfection. Indulge in gourmet meals under the African sky and take part in guided game drives, walking safaris, and boat excursions to get up close with the incredible biodiversity of the region. Whether it's observing the powerful migration of herbivores or encountering predators in action, every day promises a new discovery. This immersive safari is the ultimate way to experience Botswana’s natural wonders and its wildlife up close in an intimate, exclusive setting.

Your journey begins in the Okavango Delta, where you will explore its labyrinth of waterways teeming with diverse wildlife. Enjoy guided walking safaris, serene mokoro canoe rides, and thrilling 4x4 game drives. Experience the magic of the African bush as you spot elephants, lions, and cheetahs in their natural habitats.

The adventure continues as you move to the Savuti region, renowned for its dramatic predator-prey encounters. Your expert guides will take you on daily expeditions to witness nature in its raw, untamed beauty. At night, relax in the comfort of luxury lodges, enjoying gourmet meals prepared with local flavors.

This once-in-a-lifetime trip concludes with a visit to the Chobe River, famous for its large herds of elephants and breathtaking sunsets. Whether it’s a peaceful river cruise or a guided photographic safari, every moment is designed to leave you with unforgettable memories.

What’s Included:

  •  Luxury accommodations with all meals included
  •  Hot air balloon safari experience

  •  Airport transfers and domestic flights

What to Expect:

  •  Temperatures ranging from warm days to cool nights
  •  Opportunities for photography and wildlife observation
  •  Flexible itineraries to match your preferences
